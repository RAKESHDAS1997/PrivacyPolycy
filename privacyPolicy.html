Privacy Policy for Player

Effective Date: October 19, 2025

Player (“we”, “our”, “the app”) is an offline video player application. Player respects and protects users’ personal privacy. Please read this policy carefully before using our app.

1. How We Collect and Use Your Personal Information

Player is an offline video player. We may request access to your device storage permission to read and play video files stored locally.

  • Storage permission: Required to access your local media. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal files.
  • Player only uses this permission to enable playback and app features.

2. How We Use Cookies

Player does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Any analytics or error logging is anonymous and does not identify you personally.

3. Why We Collect Your Personal Information

  • Enable video playback from local storage
  • Improve app functionality
  • Comply with legal obligations, if applicable

4. How We Share, Transfer, and Publicly Disclose Your Personal Information

  • We do not share your personal files or data with third parties.
  • Any information shared with third-party services is anonymized.
  • We comply with legal requirements if mandated by authorities.

5. Your Rights

  • Opt-out of any personalized ads if used

6. How We Store and Protect Your Personal Information

Player does not store personal data outside your device. All media files remain on your device. We implement reasonable measures to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.
